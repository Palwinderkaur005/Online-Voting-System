Voting Guidelines

Welcome to the Voting Guidelines page! Here you will find all the essential information you need to participate in the voting process.

Eligibility Requirements

  • You must be at least 18 years old on or before Election Day.
  • You must be a citizen of the country where you are voting.
  • You must be a resident of the state in which you are voting.
  • You must be registered to vote in your state by the registration deadline.

Voting Process

Follow these steps to cast your vote:

  • Review your voter registration status.
  • Find your polling place or decide whether to vote early or absentee.
  • Research the candidates and measures on your ballot.
  • Cast your vote either in person or by mail, following all instructions carefully.
